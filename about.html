VLADIMIR VOLEGOV - russian painter

I was born on December, 19th, 1957 in Khabarovsk. The passion to drawing has begun in earliest childhood. Till 12 years old I often moved with my mother from one city of Ural Mountain to other. In 1969 family has moved to Krivoi Rog, Ukraine, where I have started to go to art school â„–1, since 14 years old I have started to earn money by drawing 3-meter posters for a cinema, 1982 I have entered in Lvov Poligraphic Institute. Since 1985 headed association of artists in Krivoi Rog, "Splav". In 1988 moved to Moscow, where have started to work in several companies at the same time, "Melodia", edition company "Plakat", advertising company "ReklamFilm", also I have painted portraits on famous street Arbat. Have made design of CD, albums covers, posters; since 1990 have started to travel to Europe, have drawn portraits on streets of Barcelona, Wien, Berlin y etc. Since 1991 have participated in project "A month of Artists" in Wels, Austria. This project was successful during 10 years. In 2004 I have started to collaborate with Weinstein Art Management, have signed contract with Soho Editions, after has started successful collaboration with Galeries Bartoux in France and also with galleries in Spain, Italy. At present time I live and work in Spain.
