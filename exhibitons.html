DOLL EXPO PARIS 2012

11 of March 2012 has passed exhibition DOLL EXPO PARIS, was coming a lot of visitors, and in spite of the idea of Fashion doll not coincided with idea of my dolls, I received many proposals and opinions. It was my first participation in exhibition and gave me confidence in creating of subsequent dolls.

ART EXPO NEW YORK 2012

I exhibited in ArtExpo NY with my paintings and showed also my first four dolls, they have had a lot of interest, because they are very different from all dolls. Two of them were bought.

You order a doll. What is required:


  • 1. Photos of the person in high quality. If there are more photos it is better. It is desirable in face and in profile.

  • 2. Your wishes and short story - a hobby, history. All this can help in doll creation.

  • 3. Exterior data: color of eyes, hair color, birthmarks, character peculiarities.

  • 4. The doll can have the built in light-emitting diodes or simple mechanical appliances.

  • 5. Discussion. Quantity, terms.

  • 6. A cooperation order:
    • a. The sketch
    • b. 50% Payment in advance
    • c. Work process in photos by e-mail
    • d. Confirmation
    • e. Payment of remained sum + sum of delivery
    • g. You have a Doll !
